Apophenia explores the psychology behind the patterns people don't realize they're repeating — why you go quiet in a full room, why you apologize before anyone's even upset, why pulling away feels safer than staying close.

Each episode breaks down one psychological archetype: the real mechanism behind it, where it usually comes from, and what actually shifts it — explained in plain language, never academic, never diagnosing anyone real.

New videos twice a week. If you've ever caught yourself thinking "why am I like this," you're probably going to recognize someone here.

Narration and illustrations are AI-assisted; psychological research, scripts, and final editing are human-curated. Not a substitute for therapy or professional mental health care.
